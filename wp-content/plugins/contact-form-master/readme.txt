== Description ==

<strong>Contact form</strong> is the most complete <strong>Contact form</strong> plugin. You can create different contact forms with different fields.

** <strong>Contact form</strong> - Fields: **

* <strong>contact form</strong> text field
* <strong>contact form</strong> number field
* <strong>contact form</strong> text area field
* <strong>contact form</strong> Address field

** <strong>Contact form</strong> - Submit options: **

* <strong>Contact form</strong> from Email
* <strong>Contact form</strong> to Email
* <strong>Contact form</strong> Subject
* <strong>Contact form</strong> Message
